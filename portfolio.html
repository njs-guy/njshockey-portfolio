Built a custom PC

CPU: Ryzen 2600x

GPU: RX 580

RAM: DDR4 16GB


I have always loved computers, but I never had the money for a good one until the summer of 2019. Building a custom PC myself really let me see what all the parts do and how they all work together. The most surprising part about it was how relatively easy the whole thing was. The only issue I ran into was some incorrectly plugged cables, which I fixed easily. It has a 500GB SATA SSD, 2TB 7200 RPM HDD, Ryzen 5 2600X, Radeon RX 580 8GB, and 16 GB of RAM. Overall, I’m really happy with this build. It can play pretty much any game I throw at it in 1080p 60 FPS at ultra to max settings.
